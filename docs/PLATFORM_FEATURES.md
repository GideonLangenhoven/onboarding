# ActivityHub — Complete Platform Features

> Everything your adventure business needs to book, manage, and grow — from your first guest to your thousandth.

---

## 1. Your Booking Website

A professional, mobile-ready booking page that sells your tours 24/7.

- **Custom branding** — your logo, colors, and hero imagery
- **Live tour catalog** — each tour shows name, price, duration, and images
- **Real-time availability** — customers see available dates and time slots instantly
- **Secure checkout** — Yoco payment integration, promo codes, and gift vouchers
- **Success page** — booking summary, calendar add links, meeting point directions, waiver signing
- **Subdomain included** — e.g. `yourbusiness.bookingtours.co.za`

---

## 2. AI Virtual Host (Web Chat + WhatsApp)

A 24/7 AI assistant that knows your business and helps guests book.

- **Answers FAQs instantly** — trained on your custom Q&A pairs
- **Guided booking flow** — walks customers through tour → date → time → guests → payment
- **Multi-channel** — works on your website chat widget AND WhatsApp
- **Custom personality** — set the AI's tone and guardrails
- **Handoff to human** — customer says "speak to a person" → routes to your inbox
- **Per-business knowledge** — each business has its own FAQ, tours, and instructions

---

## 3. Admin Dashboard

Your daily command centre for managing bookings and operations.

- **Today's manifest** — bookings grouped by time slot with pax counts
- **Check-in tracking** — mark guests as arrived, track attendance
- **Revenue summary** — today, this week, this month at a glance
- **Weather widget** — live Windguru wind/swell data for your locations
- **Action items** — unsigned waivers, pending payments, outstanding photos

---

## 4. Booking Management

Full lifecycle control from creation to completion.

- **Create manual bookings** — for walk-ins, phone bookings, agent reservations
- **Apply discounts** — promo codes, vouchers, manual overrides
- **Send payment links** — customer receives email with Yoco checkout link
- **Mark as paid** — for cash/EFT payments, triggers confirmation + invoice
- **Edit bookings** — change name, phone, email, guest count
- **Reschedule** — move to different slot (handles upgrade/downgrade payments)
- **Cancel with refund** — queues refund automatically
- **Booking detail page** — full history, waiver status, invoice, payment method

---

## 5. Schedule & Slot Management

Visual calendar for managing your tour schedule.

- **Week and day views** — see all slots at a glance
- **Generate recurring slots** — pick date range, days, times, capacity
- **Edit capacity** — single slot or bulk update
- **Peak pricing** — set seasonal price overrides by date range
- **Close/reopen slots** — for weather, maintenance, private events
- **Occupancy bars** — visual fill indicator per slot

---

## 6. Payment Processing

Secure payment handling with multiple methods.

- **Yoco integration** — credit card payments via checkout links
- **Manual payments** — mark cash/EFT as paid
- **Payment link generation** — send payment email to customer
- **Server-side price verification** — prevents frontend tampering
- **Promo code validation** — checks active, dates, uses, min order, per-email uniqueness
- **Voucher redemption** — deducts from balance, handles partial use
- **Combined discounts** — promo + voucher stack correctly

---

## 7. Refund Management

Process refunds quickly and fairly.

- **Refund queue** — all pending refunds in one view
- **Yoco refunds** — automatic API reversal for card payments
- **Manual EFT** — mark for bank transfer processing
- **Decline with reason** — customer notified
- **Batch refund** — process all pending at once
- **Voucher refunds** — cancelled voucher-paid bookings get a new voucher

---

## 8. Customer Communication

Automated messages at every touchpoint.

- **Booking confirmation** — email + WhatsApp with ref, details, waiver link
- **Day-before reminder** — WhatsApp: "Your tour is tomorrow, arrive 15 min early"
- **Waiver reminder** — email sent if waiver unsigned before trip
- **Post-trip review request** — WhatsApp with Google/TripAdvisor link
- **Cancellation notification** — email + WhatsApp with self-service options
- **Payment link email** — for pending bookings
- **Trip photos** — email + WhatsApp with gallery link
- **Re-engagement** — "It's been a while" message for dormant customers

---

## 9. Gift Vouchers

Sell and manage gift vouchers for your tours.

- **Create vouchers** — fixed amount or percentage
- **Customer purchase** — on your booking site with payment
- **Redemption at checkout** — customer enters code, balance deducted
- **Partial redemption** — remaining balance tracked, balance email sent
- **Expiry enforcement** — expired vouchers blocked automatically
- **Cancellation vouchers** — issued instead of card refund when appropriate

## 10. Waiver / Indemnity System

Digital waiver signing before every trip.

- **Waiver link in confirmation** — email + WhatsApp include signing link
- **Online form** — customer fills name, optional ID, consent checkboxes
- **Minor support** — guardian fields appear if age < 18
- **Status tracking** — admin sees SIGNED/PENDING with timestamp
- **Auto-reminders** — cron sends waiver email day before trip
- **Waiver CTA on success page** — prominent "Sign Waiver Now" button

## 11. Customer Self-Service (My Bookings)

Let customers manage their own bookings.

- **OTP login** — enter email + phone, receive 6-digit code
- **View all bookings** — status, tour, date, actions
- **Reschedule** — pick new slot (upgrade payment if needed)
- **Cancel** — with refund/voucher options
- **Rate limited** — OTP requests throttled for security

## 12. Weather Operations

Monitor conditions and cancel with confidence.

- **Live Windguru widget** — wind speed, swell, temperature forecast
- **Multiple locations** — configure spots for your area
- **Weather cancellation** — close slot, auto-cancel all bookings
- **Bulk cancel** — cancel multiple slots for a stormy day
- **Customer notifications** — email + WhatsApp with reschedule/refund options
- **Reopen** — slots back to OPEN when weather clears

## 15. Email Marketing Platform

Built-in email campaigns, contacts, and automations.

### Contacts
- **Import CSV/Excel** — 3-step: upload → map columns → preview & import
- **Data cleaning** — auto-lowercase emails, capitalize names, normalize phones
- **Tagging** — segment contacts with custom tags
- **Validate & Segment** — auto-tag VIP, unengaged, new contacts
- **Full pagination** — browse thousands of contacts

### Email Builder
- **13 block types** — text, image, button, divider, spacer, header, social, video, quote, columns, countdown, tour card, footer
- **Rich text editor** — font family, size, weight, color, alignment, bold/italic/underline
- **Branded social icons** — Facebook, Instagram, X, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, WhatsApp
- **Drag & drop** — reorder blocks with grip handle
- **Desktop/mobile preview** — see exactly how it looks
- **Template variables** — {first_name}, {promo_code}, {voucher_code}, etc.

### Campaigns
- **Send immediately or schedule** — pick date/time for future delivery
- **Audience filtering** — all contacts, or filter by tags
- **Batch delivery** — sent in batches of 50 via Resend
- **Open tracking** — pixel-based, shows in campaign analytics
- **Click tracking** — link rewriting with redirect
- **Unsubscribe** — one-click unsubscribe in every email

### Automations
- **Triggers** — contact added, tag added, post-booking, birthday/anniversary, manual
- **Steps** — send email, delay, condition check, generate voucher, generate promo
- **Drip sequences** — welcome series, post-booking follow-up, birthday rewards

### Promo Codes
- **Create codes** — flat (R) or percent (%) discounts
- **Auto-generate** — random codes with one click
- **Validation rules** — date range, max uses, min order, per-email limit
- **Usage tracking** — see used/max count in real-time
- **Works everywhere** — web checkout, admin booking, WhatsApp, external API

## 19. Security & Access Control

Enterprise-grade security for your data.

- **Role-based access** — ADMIN, MAIN_ADMIN, SUPER_ADMIN
- **Password lockout** — 5 wrong attempts = 30 min lockout + unlock email
- **Encrypted credentials** — WhatsApp/Yoco keys stored with AES-256
- **Row Level Security** — every database query scoped to business_id
- **Security headers** — X-Frame-Options, Content-Type, XSS Protection
- **HMAC webhook verification** — Yoco + Paysafe webhooks verified
- **Idempotent payments** — duplicate webhooks safely ignored
